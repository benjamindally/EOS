Biography

Megan Rosalie Kraft's ecclectic talents and tastes showcase her enduring respect and love for music. Working as a composer, lyracist, singer, and producer, Rosalie has infused dynamic passion into her art. Classically trained in voice along with studies in global compositions, she has in-depth understanding of theory and vocal production.

Rosalie has experience working in multiple genres and is comfortable working on a variety of mediums such as film, dance, or advertisement. Of her many talents, she primarily works in electronic pop using Logic Pro X and Ableton Digital Audio Workstations. Based out of San Diego, Megan Rosalie Kraft is able to travel to Los Angelos or Orange County and is interested in working in many capacities. Contact Rosalie for her music services.
